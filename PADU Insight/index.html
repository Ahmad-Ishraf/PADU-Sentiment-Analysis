Pengkalan Data Utama (PADU)


Pengkalan Data Utama more commonly known as PADU is an initiative in Malaysia that aims to enhance government services and decision-making. PADU serves as a centralised database hub that integrates information from various government departments and agencies. Its primary purpose is to provide a clearer picture of national household income. The government intends to use PADU for effective subsidy distribution and informed policy-making.

Reasoning

The reasoning behind this study is due to the lack of clear public sentiment regarding PADU. The method that is planned to be used in this study is Bidirectional Encoder Representations from Transformers (BERT).

From this, valuable insights into how citizens perceive this government initiative can be seen.

How

This study starts with data collection by scraping X for tweets related to PADU which is then cleaned and fed into a fine-tuned BERT model in order to ascertain the sentiment of each tweet, whether they are Negative, Neutral or Positive in sentiment.

The data gained from this is then visualized into charts and graph to better represent and convey the result.
