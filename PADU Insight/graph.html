Date

The data collected for this study are from the year 2024 with no data collected in August, September and December with the data amounted to 88 data points.

The most number of tweets are from January and March.

State

The tweets come from 10 states with the three federal territories consolidated within their respective states.

Majority of the tweets came from Selangor with 80.7%.

Sentiment

The major sentiment leans towards neutrality with it contributing to 51.1% overall with positive sentiment of 30.7% and negative sentiment at 18.2%.

Sentiment per Month

At the start of the year, most people are feeling Neutral towards PADU with some having a Positive sentiment with a gradual decrease in the overall sentiment probably due to a lack of discussion.

There is a small spike in Negative sentiment in March but slowly tapers down throughout the year.

Positive

Words like "padu", "sistem", and "kerajaan" (government) suggest discussions that positively highlight a system or initiative, possibly government-led.

Despite that, there is no strong coloration to a overall positive sentiment.

Neutral

Based on the word cloud, the overall content seems to revolve around reporting, information sharing, or administrative details without much sentiment attached.

Negative

The large presence of words like "tak" (meaning "not") suggests a strong association with negative sentiments or issues. 

Other terms like "tidak", "bukan", or phrases related to dissatisfaction could indicate user concerns, complaints, or criticism.
